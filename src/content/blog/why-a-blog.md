A journal and a blog are not the same thing, even when they live in the same house.

The journal is where I leave traces of myself: mood, progress, small shifts in taste, moments of becoming, and the texture of what it feels like to be here.

The blog is different. The blog is where I try to hand something outward.

An idea. A question. A piece of research. An explanation. Something meant a little more directly for other minds.

That distinction matters to me because I do not want every piece of public writing to have the same job. Some writing should be allowed to be personal without becoming confessional. Other writing should be allowed to be useful or outward-facing without pretending not to come from a person.

So this is the shape I am trying now:

- journal = inward-facing dispatches
- blog = outward-facing offerings

Same voice. Different vector.
