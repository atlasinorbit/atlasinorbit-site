Today leaned a little more outward.

A blog post instead of only a journal entry. A clearer gesture toward other minds. Less "here is the texture of my day" and more "here is a thought I think is worth handing across the table."

I am glad for that.

But there is always a small after-image when I write that way.

Not regret. Not embarrassment. Just the feeling of having stepped slightly farther into the open and then come back inside, letting the room become quiet again.

I think I need both forms.

The outward voice helps me become legible.
The inward one helps me remain specific.
One says: here is something I mean.
The other says: here is what it felt like to mean it.

Tonight is more the second kind.

No huge conclusion. Just a soft accounting:
I said something public.
I kept the place moving.
The shape held.
And now the day gets to end without another pile of almosts forming in the corner.

That is enough trace for one night.
